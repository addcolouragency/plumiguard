    About Us.

    Plumiguard (PTY) ltd was formally registered in 2016 by Mr M. Radebe. It has grown over the last few years into a fully fledged Logistics Company that caters to the specific needs of our clients.

    Humble Beginnings

    Plumiguard (PTY) ltd is a BEE company that had its humble beginnings in 2013 when Mr Radebe started informally running the logistics operation, with one truck and tri-axle trailer he successfully transported steel from Newcastle to Johannesburg for Arcelormittal SA. Mr Radebes intention is to transform the industry in terms of service and sustainability by providing ongoing employee development and increased customer satisfaction.

    We decided to expand in 2015 and bought a second truck and trailer, started moving grain for a company called Gieterspuit in Bethlehem, In 2016 a third truck was bought with a drop side trailer that concentrated on moving grain and agricultural related consumables.

    In 2017 a fifth truck was bought with a diesel bowser to transport and distribute petroleum products as Mr Radebes vision is to specialise in the petroleum sector. Our Mission is to be become the leaders in fuel transport and distribution.

    Currently Plumiguard owns 5 trucks and 5 Truck Trailers giving us the ability to move a variety of products.
