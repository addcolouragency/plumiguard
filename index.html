Water Tanker

We have a fleet of water tankers in various sizes up to 14000 litres. All our tankers are fully equipped to load themselves from water sources and can be discharged where required. A fully qualified driver is available for all our tankers and our service is friendly, reliable and punctual.

Transport

Our tipper trucks are in great condition and extremely reliable and can be hired on a per day basis as well as for short or long term periods.

Our range of trucks include 6 cube and 10 cube tipper trucks which are ideal for moving gravel, rubble, sand, dirt, trees, garden refuse or even building materials.  We offer a fully qualified driver for all our tipper trucks and our service is friendly, reliable and punctual. Our tipper trucks are equipped with Copelyn trailers allowing the loads to be easily dumped out of the back at the required site.

About Us.

Plumiguard (PTY) Ltd was formally registered in 2016 by Mr M. Radebe. It has grown over the last few years into a fully fledged Logistics Company that caters to the specific needs of our clients.

Humble Beginnings

Plumiguard (PTY) ltd is a BEE company that had its humble beginnings in 2013 when Mr Radebe started informally running the logistics operation, with one truck and tri-axle trailer he successfully transported steel from Newcastle to Johannesburg for Arcelormittal SA. Mr Radebe’s intention is to transform the industry in terms of service and sustainability by providing ongoing employee development and increased customer satisfaction.

We decided to expand in 2015 and bought a second truck and trailer, started moving grain for a company called Gieterspuit in Bethlehem, In 2016 a third truck was bought with a drop side trailer that concentrated on moving grain and agricultural related consumables.

In 2017 a fifth truck was bought with a diesel bowser to transport and distribute petroleum products as Mr Radebe’s vision is to specialise in the petroleum sector. Our Mission is to be become the leaders in fuel transport and distribution.

Currently Plumiguard owns 5 trucks and 5 Truck Trailers giving us the ability to move a variety of products.
